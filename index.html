About Us

Welcome to Sethi Jewellers, a beacon of elegance and craftsmanship since 1984. With a legacy spanning four decades, we pride ourselves on unparalleled quality, exquisite designs, and a commitment to offering the finest in gold and diamond jewellery. Specializing in 22 karat gold, we set the standard for the lowest rates in the tricity, making fine jewellery accessible to all.



Established in 1984, Sethi Jewellers is synonymous with excellence in jewellery craftsmanship. Our diverse range caters to varied tastes, from the timeless allure of diamonds to the warmth of 22 karat gold. Each piece, be it rings, necklaces, earrings, karas, bracelets, or creations with precious and semi-precious stones, embodies a perfect fusion of traditional charm and contemporary elegance.


What sets us apart is not just the brilliance of our jewels but our unwavering commitment to quality and superior customer service. Purchasing jewellery is a deeply personal journey, and our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you. Our history is a testament to the trust and confidence our customers have placed in us over the years. At Sethi Jewellers, we invite you to explore a journey of timeless beauty, where every piece tells a story since 1984.
