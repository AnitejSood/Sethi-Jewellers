About

Welcome to Sethi Jewellers, a beacon of elegance and craftsmanship that has adorned the tricity since 1984. With a legacy spanning nearly four decades, we take pride in offering unparalleled quality, exquisite designs, and a commitment to providing our valued customers with the finest in gold and diamond jewellery.


Established in 1984, Sethi Jewellers has been a trailblazer in the industry, specializing in 22 karat gold and offering the lowest rates in the entire tricity. Our unique selling proposition underscores our dedication to making fine jewellery not only exquisite but also accessible to all, ensuring our customers acquire pieces of exceptional value.


Our diverse product range reflects a keen understanding of varied tastes and preferences. From the timeless allure of diamonds to the warmth of 22 karat gold, our collection includes rings, necklaces, earrings, karas, bracelets, and precious & semi-precious stone creations. Each piece is meticulously crafted to embody the perfect fusion of traditional charm and contemporary elegance.


What truly sets us apart is not just the brilliance of our jewels but the unwavering commitment to quality and superior customer service that defines the Sethi Jewellers experience. We understand that purchasing jewellery is a deeply personal and meaningful journey, and our knowledgeable and friendly staff is here to guide you every step of the way. Our history is a testament to the trust and confidence our customers have placed in us over the years
